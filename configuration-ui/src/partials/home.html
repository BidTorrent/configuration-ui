Host a RTB in your browser

BidTorrent is a browser-hosted RTB allowing you to cut third party fees and have full control on the bid process

I'm a publisher
I'm a bidder

Full transparency

The BidTorrent protocol is public and its configuration is based on OpenRTB Specifications.The auction client can be found on github, it is fully open source so don't hesitate to fork it on github!

Less intermediaries

Bidtorrent will allow you to call directly the bidders, run the ads auction client side and doing so, avoid RTB fees.

Full control

As a publisher, you can easily create your own configuration on BidTorrent.io. BidTorrent gives you full control on the way you want to handle the auction and provide you the classic RTB features.

Plug & Play

Child's play to integrate! Just plug BidTorrent script directly to your page.

By creating your configuration on our website only one javascript line is required to be live!
